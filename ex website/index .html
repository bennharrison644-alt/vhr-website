About VHR

Veterans Humanitarian Response (VHR) is a volunteer rapid-response NGO made up of veterans offering emergency medical care, logistics, security coordination, and communications support to communities affected by conflict and crisis.

  • Mission: Quickly and responsibly support lifesaving operations where civilian infrastructure and medical access are disrupted.
  • Values: Integrity • Speed • Coordination • Humanity
  • Composition: Medical officers, logistics coordinators, security advisors, and communications specialists — 11-person international team.

Operations

Our approach focuses on short-term deployments to stabilise medical needs, establish safe supply lines, and restore communications for local responders. We prioritise working with local partners and adhering to humanitarian principles.

Rapid Deployment

Pre-packed medical kits, rapid logistics staging, and experienced security oversight.

Medical Support

Emergency trauma care, triage, and patient stabilization for referrals to local hospitals.

Communications & Coordination

Secure comms, coordination with NGOs and local authorities, and situational reporting.
